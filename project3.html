Impact Measurement Device


Design and implimentation of a low cost, high frequency load cell system while working for the OSU Prototype Development Lab.
https://research.engr.oregonstate.edu/protodevlab/
Low cost system designed to reach a minimum of 2000 load cell readings per second, per client's request.

Electronics Design:

  • Three seperate Teensy 4.0 microcontrollers for increased throughput
  • External Analog-to-digital-conveter module for up to 30k samples per second
  • Calibration sequence and plotting using pyserial to compile information from the three microcontrollers


Mechanical Design:

  • Load cells sized for maximum force with a 1.5 Factor of Safety
  • Housing designed for electronics in Solidworks
