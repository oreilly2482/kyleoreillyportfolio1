Drill Press Table

During my internship at Ascentec Engineering I designed a table for their newly purchased drill press.
To reduce cost the table was designed around dimensions of square tube steel the shop had as scrap.



Mechanical Design:
  • Designed in Fusion360
  • Parts manufactured out of steel using bandsaw and grinder
  • Included shelf for shop vac, as well as peg board for tool bits per floor worker's requests
